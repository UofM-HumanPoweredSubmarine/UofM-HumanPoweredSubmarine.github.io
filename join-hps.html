If you want to purchase material by comparing properties and assessing requirements,
If you want to manufacture a mold and fiberglass hull,

If you want to manage the expenses to increase efficiencies in a large-scale engineering operation,

If you want to meet passionate, fun-loving, and like-minded individuals,

You have a place at Michigan HPS.

    2. The Michigan Daily

    "If you break out singing the Beatles’ “Yellow Submarine” during a meeting of the University’s Human Powered Submarine team, prepare for some backlash.
