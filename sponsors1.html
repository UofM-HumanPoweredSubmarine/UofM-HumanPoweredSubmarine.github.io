Written Documentation (all donations)

In order to receive a tax deduction for your donation, please remit payment with a simple letter including:
  • Donor's intent to make a gift
  • How the gift is intended to be used (i.e. for the HPS team, etc)
  • Donor's signature
  • For material donations: Written appraisal of the gift's value. If gift's value is over $5,000, an independent, third-party appraisal will be required. Sales tax should not be included in this value.
